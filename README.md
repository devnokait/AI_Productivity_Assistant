AI Productivity Assistant(Workflow AI)
Overview
Workflow AI is an AI-powered productivity assistant tool designed to automate commonplace tasks through a single integrated application.
The application integrates four AI-assisted productivity tools into one centralised workspace,aiding users in improving communication,efficiency,planning and overall information management.
The application features a smart email generator which generates professional workplace emails based on the audience,purpose,tone and additional instructions.It supports three main audiences,namely: client,manager and team member.It also supports three tones,namely:formal,informal and persuasive.
It also features a meeting notes summarizer,which converts lengthy meeting notes into concise summaries.The outputs include:executive summary,key discussion points,decision made,action items and deadlines.
An AI task planner & scheduler is also included which helps users organise workloads by :prioritising tasks,managing deadlines,generating daily schedules,generating daily schedules and providing productivity recommendations.
The last feature also included is an AI research assistant, which simplifies research by: summarising topics,extracting key insights,providing recommendations and highlighting important takeaways.
The Workflow AI makes use of a modern glassmorphism inspired design language.It has a transparent pastel blue theme, frosted glass cards, rounded user interface components ,responsive layouts,mobile and desktop compatibility,smooth transitions and animations and a clean navigation structure.
In the application,responsible AI principles.The AI disclaimer message used was:AI-generated content may contain inaccuracies and should always be reviewed before use.Users have been warned not to share confidential business information,sensitive personal data,private organisational information.
There is a variety of technologies that were used,the list includes:Frontend Technologies(React,TypeScript,Vite,Tailwinf CSS),Development Tools(Lovable AI,GitHub,Visual Studio Code),Deployment(Loveable Hosting Platform)
